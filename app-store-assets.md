## Long Description (App Store / Play Store)

Bible Guide AI is your personal AI-powered Bible study companion, designed to deepen your faith through daily devotionals, guided prayers, and intelligent Scripture exploration.

**Daily Devotionals**
Start each day with a fresh, AI-generated devotional that includes a Bible verse, reflection, and prayer. Listen to devotionals hands-free with built-in text-to-speech audio.

**AI Bible Chat**
Ask any question about the Bible and receive thoughtful, verse-backed answers powered by advanced AI. Experience real-time streaming responses that feel like a conversation with a knowledgeable study partner.

**Reading Plans**
Follow structured Bible reading plans like "Foundations of Faith" and "Psalms of Comfort" to build consistent study habits. Track your progress and maintain daily streaks.

**Prayer Journal**
Record your prayers, track answered prayers, and build a meaningful prayer life with guided prompts and reflection tools.

**Community**
Share testimonies, post verse reflections, submit prayer requests, and join prayer groups. Support fellow believers on their faith journey.

**Personalized Experience**
Choose your interests during onboarding, set daily reminder times, and enjoy the app in English, Indonesian, or Arabic (with full RTL support).

**Premium Features**
Unlock unlimited AI conversations, advanced reading plans, and exclusive content with Bible Guide AI Premium.

**Built for Your Lifestyle**
- Dark mode for comfortable nighttime reading
- Offline access to cached devotionals and reading plans
- Push notification reminders for daily devotions
- Beautiful glassmorphism UI with smooth animations
- Full accessibility support

Download Bible Guide AI today and transform your daily walk with God.
